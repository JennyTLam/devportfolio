About

After a quarter filled with readings, discussions, conversations, and reflections on how we can learn from our experiences and build on those of others to better design our lives, I've put together a series of single page portfolios for each of the three versions of myself as part of a final design showcase project. This is an opportunity to take a deeper look into the possibilities for the next five years, how I might grow in each Odyssey Plan, and also to finally start compiling a personal portfolio to share some of the projects I've worked on.

Odyssey Plans

Software Engineer

"What is the plan you've always had?" As a computer science major, a career in software engineering has been the go to post-graduation plan. From internships to a range of computing projects, most of my time has been dedicated to finding a role as a software engineer with a focus in augmented reality applications and wearable tech.

View Plan

UI/UX Designer

"What is the plan if plan A no longer exists?" If there was no more need for software engineers, I'd delve deeper into user interface and experience design. It's important that the things we create are made with a human user in mind, whether that be in its interactions, aesthetics, or functionalities. I'm interested in accessible technology and gaining insights into how others view the world and the ways things work.

View Plan

Startup Founder

"What is the plan if money were no object?" Launching a startup is no easy task, but certainly a rewarding option if I want to explore ideas and emerging use cases for newer technologies. From incubator to pitch after pitch, founding a startup presents endless challenges to tackle and lots of flexibility in terms of direction and work-life.
