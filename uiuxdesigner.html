About

Welcome to a glimpse of my world! My name is Jenny Lam and I'm a UI/UX Designer at Relativity. I'm interested in human-centered application design and development with a focus on augmented reality and wearable tech. In my free time, you can find me sketching designs, perusing the Muse, brainstorming, and turning expectations into reality.

Projects

Daybreak

Daybreak is a mobile application that helps users take a break from everyday stressors and reflect on their accomplishments, goals, events of the day, and plan for what tomorrow might look like. Through a series of journal prompts and progress visualization, Daybreak allows users to celebrate memories and practice self-care.

CarGo

CarGo is an Android application that helps users find carpools and calculate rideshare expenses among riders based on distance travelled and local gas prices. CarGo taps into the SmartCar API to obtain accurate odometer information to track different trips.

Design Sprints

Monthly virtual workshops in UI/UX and graphic design with interested high school students to go over brainstorming and prototyping strategies and put those skills into practice through incrementally improving interfaces for student-led technical projects.
